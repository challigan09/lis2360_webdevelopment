Dean Kamen

Without a doubt, Dean Kamen is a true visionary of the future. Dean is a inventor, entrepreneur, and advocate of science and technology. As of today, he current holds more than 440 U.S. and foreign patents. Most of his patents are for innovative medical devices. his first invention, while in college I might add, was a wearable infusion pump. In 1976, he founded his own company, AutoSyringe, inc., to manufacture and market the pumps. He went on to create devices which help in mobility, diabetes, insulin pumps, and most popular of them all, the segway.

Cloud Computing By no means is this a trend.

Cloud computing has been around for a good minute. The trend however lies in the number of industries that are relying more and more on cloud computing. The benefits of the cloud are mobility, reliability, and in most cases, security. Cloud computing reduces the cost of software installations and IT support. Essentially, cloud computing has completely changed computng infrasucture and the technology is still in its infancy stage. The potential that lies with this trend is truly mind blowing!
